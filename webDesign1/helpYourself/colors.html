About


This color pallette was made in the Tali 1st floor. My goal was to have a variety of shades of blue to give a cool and vibrant website color. I used some shades from a reflction of the sun off blue surfaces and also used a shade of blue from Mr. Sternitzky's shirt. For the darker colors I chose a spot between a blue and black to specifically pick the shade.
