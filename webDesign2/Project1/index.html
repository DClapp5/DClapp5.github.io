Maui

If you are planning a trip to hawaii, I would definitely reccomend maui. The beaches there are beautiful and it is all around a very relaxing and enjoyable experience no matter how you spend your time there.




Ziplining

One of my favorite things I have done in Maui is ziplining. The feeling as you glide over the thick green forests, almost feeling like you are flying, is something that I feel there is no comparrison to. If you enjoy ziplining in general, then you should definitely add it to your list of stuff to do in hawaii.
