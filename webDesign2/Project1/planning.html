Planning

Twin Falls

Twin falls is an amazing waterfall at the end of a short hike (~20 minutes). You can swim under the waterfall or stay out of the water and watch, but either way the experience is amazing and the sightseeing on the hike is great. You wont regret taking the time to do this hike, but you may regret not swimming at the end of it.



Haleakala

Haleakala is one of the most amazing sigts in Maui. You get to watch a sunrise from the top of a large mountain, but make sure you book way ahead of time because its very hard to get tickets for. While seeing this requires you to get up early, it is definitely worth it.
